About Mwiko

I fell in love with writing code at a young age because i wanted to be able to build my own video games, at that time i just wanted to be able to create my own snake, tetris, sonic or super mario so i began to do some research on the web and there i read about how to get started with python for dummies which led me to writing a lot of really simple 2D games in pygame around my high school years. In college i started a side hustle of building websites which at the time was mostly business profiles for local small businesses and non governmental organizations.. ever since those days then i have been doing a lot of freelance works while also trying to find more interesting projects to collaborate on.. I've always had the curiosity and desire to build random stuff and very much enjoy the process of putting the pieces together. My organization and attention to detail are outstanding, but my main strength is in spotting problems and solving them, you can see some of my more creative problem solving in my work.
